Books

  • The Design of Everyday Things by Don Norman This is definitely THE starter book for understanding UX Design. Definitely check out this book by Don.
  • UX for Beginners by Joel Marsh A sweet beginners guide to UX.
  • The Design Studio Method by Brian Sullivan Great book for understanding how to work within a Design team.
  • UX Methods: A Quick Guide to User Experience Research Methods by James Pannafino Everything you need to know about UX Design research methods and usability tests.
  • Designing for People: An Introduction to Human Factors Engineering by John D Lee, Christopher D. Wickens, Yili Liu, Linda Ng Boyle This book is a bit dry, but well worth the read. Packed full of tons of great information on how to account for humans when designing anything and everything.
